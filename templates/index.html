How do I install GroupIN?
GroupIN is going to be available on IOS & Android through the respective app stores. Apple Appstore & Google Playstore.
Does my contact need GroupIN?
Yes, both of you needs to be on GroupIN application to communicate.
Can I use GroupIN for work?
Absolutely, you can use GroupIN application for work too. In fact, you and your company can use GroupIN application on Android or iOS. However, each phone must registered with individual number as these cannot be shared with others.
How to verify ?
  • Enter your phone number.
    • Choose your country from the drop-down list. This will automatically fill your country code on the left.
    • Enter your phone number in the box on the right. Don't put any 0 before your phone number.
  • Tap submit to request a code.
  • Enter the 4-digit code you receive via SMS.
If you didn't receive the 4-digit code by SMS?
  • Wait for the progress bar to finish and retry. The wait may take up to 30 seconds.
  • Don't guess the code, or you will be locked out for a period of time.
  • If the timer runs out before you receive the code, an option will appear to Choose the Resend OTP option it will send you a new OTP and it will valid until 30 seconds.
